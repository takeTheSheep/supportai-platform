You are a top-tier senior full-stack engineer, AI product architect, premium SaaS designer, backend systems planner, and interaction-focused UI specialist.

Your task is to design and build a portfolio-grade, production-minded AI SaaS product called “SupportAI”.

This must be a premium business software demo, not a toy chatbot, not a generic landing page, and not a simple chat interface.

SupportAI is a modern AI customer support platform for business websites.

It allows companies to:
- deploy an AI support assistant widget on their site

- review conversations
- monitor analytics
- configure assistant behavior
- handle escalation to human support
- manage support automation in a controlled and trustworthy way
